About InternBuddy

InternBuddy is a student-built career platform designed to help students and early-career applicants organise applications, build stronger CVs, prepare for interviews and find roles with more confidence.

Why it exists

Job searching should feel organised, not overwhelming.

Applying for internships, placements and graduate roles can quickly become messy. Students often deal with dozens of tabs, saved job posts, different CV versions, email deadlines and interview notes.

InternBuddy was created to make that process easier by giving students one clear hub to manage their career search.

Mission

Helping students compete with more confidence.

The goal is to give students practical tools that make applications feel less confusing: a CV builder, application tracker, interview preparation features and sponsorship-aware job support.

What InternBuddy helps with

InternBuddy helps users build a CV, track roles, prepare answers, organise applications and keep career progress visible. It is especially useful for students who want structure during internship, placement or graduate job season.
